Choosing a study abroad destination is one of the most important decisions in your academic journey. Start by defining your career goals, budget, and preferred program structure.

## Compare more than rankings

Look at employability, internship access, post-study work rights, and living costs — not just university rankings.

## Match your profile

Shortlist countries where your academic background, English proficiency, and finances align with typical admission and visa requirements.

## Get expert review

A structured counselling session can help you avoid expensive mistakes and build a realistic timeline.
