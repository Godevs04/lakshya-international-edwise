Visa officers assess whether your study plan is genuine, funded, and aligned with your background.

## Be concise and consistent

Explain why you chose the university, program, and country in simple, factual language.

## Know your finances

Be ready to explain tuition sources, living expenses, sponsor details, and loan sanction letters if applicable.

## Practice mock interviews

Mock sessions help reduce anxiety and improve clarity under pressure.
