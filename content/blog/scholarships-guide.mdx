Scholarships can significantly reduce your study abroad cost if you plan early.

## Start 12 months ahead

Many scholarships close before admission deadlines. Build a calendar of opportunities by country and university.

## Tailor each application

Customize essays and recommendation focus areas for each scholarship instead of reusing one generic application.

## Combine funding sources

Students often combine partial scholarships, education loans, and family support for a complete funding plan.
