Education loans can fund tuition, living expenses, and travel when planned correctly. Lenders typically evaluate admission proof, co-applicant income, and collateral (if applicable).

## Core documents

Keep your admission letter, fee structure, KYC documents, academic transcripts, and income proof ready before applying.

## Compare lenders early

Interest rates, moratorium periods, and processing timelines vary. Compare at least two to three lenders before finalizing.

## Track sanction to disbursement

After sanction, coordinate disbursement schedules with your university fee deadlines to avoid admission delays.
