Your Statement of Purpose (SOP) should connect your past academics, current motivation, and future goals.

## Recommended structure

1. Academic background and key projects  
2. Why this program and university  
3. Career goals after graduation  
4. Why you are a strong fit

## Avoid generic statements

Replace broad claims with specific examples, outcomes, and skills gained from projects or work experience.

## Review before submission

Proofread for grammar, consistency, and alignment with your resume and recommendation letters.
